Mary Kuhner and Jon Yamato's Home Page

Mary Kuhner and Jon Yamato work in population and evolutionary genetics at the University of Washington, Seattle. We are co-authors of the program package LAMARC which contains programs to estimate parameters such as population size, mutation rate, growth rate, and recombination rate from molecular samples taken from a population.

Until the University clarifies its policy on faculty web pages, you will have to look elsewhere for information on our non-academic interests and activities.

Mary and Jon support the In Defense of Science initiative.
